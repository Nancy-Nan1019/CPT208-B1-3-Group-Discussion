AI Usage Summary
How AI supported the portfolio process.

AI helped us accelerate early ideation, improve wording clarity, compare layout options, and polish sections such as reflection, references, and presentation text. Final decisions, structure, and editing were always reviewed and adjusted by the team. No core design logic or user research findings were generated by AI - these remained our original work.

AI Reflection
What we learned from using AI.

We reflected on both the benefits and limitations of AI assistance while keeping human judgement in control of the final output.

What worked

AI was useful for speeding up early drafts, generating alternative phrasings, and helping us organise content more efficiently across the portfolio.

What we learned

We learned that AI should support the team rather than replace thinking. Every output needed checking, editing, and adaptation to fit the project goals and our own design decisions. We also ensured that all AI-generated text or structure suggestions were reviewed for accuracy, tone, and relevance before being included in the final portfolio.
