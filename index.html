User Personas

Created using the 10-80-10 Rule: Human Direction (10%) + AI Generation (80%) + Human Refinement (10%)

Target User: Student

Chloe (The Silent Contributor)

Bio: A high-achieving but introverted student who often stays quiet in fast-paced discussions to avoid judgment or interruption.

Pain Points: Dominant peers taking over; anxiety about verbal expression; ideas being "lost" in the noise.

System Solution: Provides an anonymous, text-based platform for her to contribute ideas without the pressure of speaking.

Target User: Teacher

Dr. Lin (The Data-Driven Educator)

Bio: A passionate tutor who wants to move away from "black box" discussions and gain real-time insights into student engagement.

Goals: To facilitate inclusive discussions and collect structured data/feedback from every group member efficiently.

System Solution: Offers a visualization dashboard that tracks participation levels and summarizes key discussion themes for better facilitation.
